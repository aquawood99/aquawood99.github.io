Information We Collect

We need specific information from your Facebook account when you play our Facebook Instant Games. Facebook gives us a unique player ID for each game, linked to your personal details like your name, profile picture, and language.

When creating personalized jigsaw puzzles using your Facebook profile picture, we collect data from the image, including the location of facial features and different facial regions, but we don’t use this data to identify or authenticate individuals, nor share this data with third parties.

Storage of User Data

We store your personal data only as long as necessary for our processing purposes or as required by law. If you're inactive for 90 days, we'll automatically delete your data. However, data needed for legal reasons is kept until the law no longer requires us to hold it, after which it's deleted promptly, unless specific legal exceptions apply.

If you've removed our Service, your entire play history and data are gone. Should you find it challenging to delete your data, feel free to reach out to us at support@aquawood.org. To help us locate and remove your data, please include the name of the Facebook Instant Game and your Facebook Instant Game Player ID in your email. We'll take care of the deletion immediately upon receiving your request.

You can request to delete all your data by contacting us at support@aquawood.org. We will consider and act upon any request in accordance with applicable data protection laws. If you have more questions, please contact us at support@aquawood.org.

Manage Data Access via Facebook

To manage what information our Services access via Facebook, follow Facebook's steps to update your privacy settings. You can always remove a game from your list of active Instant Games by visiting Facebook's application settings. When you stop giving our Services access to your data, we can't collect more data or send you messages anymore. You can delete your game history on Facebook for our Services:

  1. Go to Facebook's application settings.
  2. Select the game you wish to remove and click "Remove".
  3. In the confirmation popup, tick the box to delete your game history, then click "Remove" again.
  4. Click on "View Removed Apps and Websites" link.
  5. In the popup, click "View" next to the app.
  6. In the next window, click "Send Request" to delete your data.

Push Notification

Users receive updates and relevant content about our Services via messages from Facebook Game Messenger Bots, known as "Push Notifications."

These Push Notifications are delivered via Facebook Messenger with the user's prior consent. The user's browser, app, and/or device must support these notifications.

For us to send Push Notifications, we need to store certain information from the first time you play the game, including your game-scoped ID, Facebook Messenger user ID, language preferences, and the timestamp of your last game session. At any point, users can opt out of receiving Push Notifications by changing their settings in Facebook Messenger.
