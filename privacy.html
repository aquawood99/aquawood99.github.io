Privacy Policy

AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. – Committed to Protecting Your Privacy

1. Introduction

AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our applications and services.

This policy applies to all applications, products, and services provided by AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. These applications and services may be distributed through digital platforms such as the Apple App Store or other marketplaces.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information
    Information such as your email address or name may be collected when you contact support, create an account, or subscribe to services.
  • Device Information
    Information about the device you use including device identifiers, operating system, device model, and application version.
  • Usage Information
    Information about how you interact with our applications such as feature usage, crash reports, diagnostics, and performance analytics.
  • Authentication Data
    For certain services, encrypted authentication data may be stored locally on your device to support security-related functionality.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our applications and services.
  • To improve application performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To respond to support inquiries and user requests.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. AI Processing and Automated Technologies

Some AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. applications use artificial intelligence technologies to process content provided by users, such as text, images, documents, or audio, in order to deliver requested features.

AI processing may include:

  • Image recognition or processing
  • Text translation
  • Document conversion
  • Image generation or enhancement
  • Automated layout or design generation

User-provided content is processed solely for the purpose of generating the requested functionality. We do not use uploaded content for biometric identification or facial recognition.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help operate our services, including:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support tools

These providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for service operation.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures including:

  • Encrypted network transmission
  • Secure infrastructure
  • Access control systems
  • Monitoring for security threats

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Product-Specific Privacy Information

Certain applications or services may involve the processing of user-provided content such as photos, files, text, audio, or other materials solely for the purpose of delivering requested features and functionality.

Where applicable, uploaded content may be processed temporarily on secure servers or locally on your device. We do not sell uploaded content, and we do not use such content for biometric identification, facial recognition, profiling, or identity analysis.

For services involving authentication or security functionality, sensitive authentication-related data may be stored locally on your device in encrypted form. AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. does not have access to your passwords, authentication codes, or passkeys.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the contact information below.

10. Children's Privacy

AQUAWOOD PTE. LTD. services are not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or services. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
